Eastern Loop

If you enjoy stargazing, birding, wine tasting, and glamping, the East Loop is for you. With more frequent contact with the outside world, opportunities to resupply and to go wine tasting, as well as some unique lodging options along the way, the East Loop is slightly less committing and a little more of a luxurious experience than the West Loop.

The Route

This route is a 4 out of 10 in terms of difficulty. It travels along smooth and rocky dirt roads and sandy double tracks and is 99% rideable. With a total elevation of only 8,267 ft. over 125 miles, this is a mellow route for the reward and is great for all levels, especially for those new to bikepacking.

The Bikes

Gravel touring bikes or hard tail mountain bikes built for touring a great option for the Sky Islands. This route can be completed on 47 mm tires, but the suggested tire size is 2.0-3.0" to combat the sandy and rocky sections.
