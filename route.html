Overview

This route is a 4 out of 10 in terms of difficulty. It travels along smooth and rocky dirt roads and sandy double tracks and is 99% rideable. With a total elevation of only 8,267 ft. over 125 miles, this is a mellow route for the reward and is great for all levels, especially for those new to bikepacking.

Highlights

  • Spectacular sunrises and sunsets
  • Idyllic and easy camping throughout
  • World-class stargazing
  • Warm sunny days + cool dry nights
  • Mellow terrain and rewarding scenery
  • Diversity of flora and fauna
  • The view of the San Raphael Valley from San Raphael Valley Road
  • Permit access to ride through the preserved tall grasslands of the Appleton-Whittell Research Ranch and Babacomari Ranch.
